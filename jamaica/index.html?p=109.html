WHY REPORT DEVELOPMENTS?

By reporting developments in your area you can help improve the public’s understanding of what is happening in Jamaica. You can share concerns about possible illegal developments or major developments that can harm the environment or public health. You can also submit photos, images or videos along with your report.

HOW TO REPORT A DEVELOPMENT

You can report a development by following the simple and easy steps below.

HOW YOUR REPORTS WILL BE USED

Your report will reviewed prior to being uploaded to the website. If approved, your reported development will be displayed on the Development Alert map for all visitors to the site to see. We are not responsible for how this information is used by visitors to the website and we reserve the right not to upload reports that are inappropriate.
